# City of London Housing Repair Source Notes

Last verified: 2026-06-06

This note explains the source boundary for the Signbridge advocacy demo. The local corpus is deliberately small and source-backed for a housing repair / damp and mould appointment involving a City of London managed home.

## Verified For Demo

- City of London publishes a damp and mould reporting page with reporting details, response aims, vulnerability details to include, and evidence advice.
- City of London publishes a versioned Damp, Mould & Condensation Policy, re-approved 21 October 2025.
- GOV.UK publishes Awaab's Law guidance for tenants and social landlords, including a child-asthma damp/mould emergency example.
- City of London publishes a housing complaints and customer feedback page.
- London Datastore, Housing in London, ONS disability data, City Tenant Satisfaction Measures, and the 2026 Regulator of Social Housing judgement are verified context sources.
